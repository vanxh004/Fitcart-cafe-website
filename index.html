Our Story

The Fitkart Cafe Experience

Welcome to Fitkart Cafe And Restaurant, a friendly local cafe dedicated to serving fresh, delicious, and affordable food in the heart of New Delhi. We believe that good food shouldn't be expensive, which is why everything on our menu is priced delightfully between ₹1 and ₹200.

Whether you're a student looking for a cozy study spot, a family enjoying a weekend treat, or friends catching up over cold coffee, our warm ambiance makes everyone feel at home.

  • Perfect for Students, Families & Friends
  • 100% Fresh Ingredients Daily
  • Exceptional 5-Star Customer Service
